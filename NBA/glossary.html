3PT%- three pointer percentage. Calculated as three point field goals made divided by three point field goals attempted.

AST/TO- assists per turnover.

AST/FG- assists per field goal. Shows what percent of a team's field goals were assisted.

BLK%- block percentage. Calculated as blocks divided by opponents' possesions. Shows what percent of a team's opponents' possesions end in a block.

DRB%- defensive rebound percentage. Calculated as defensive rebounds divided by opponents' possesions. Shows what percent of a team's opponents' possesions end in a defensive rebound.

FG%- field goal percentage. Calculated as field goals made divided by field goals attempted.

FT%- free throw percentage. Calculated as free throws made divided by free throws attempted.

ORB%- offensive rebound percentage. Calculated as offensive rebounds divided by possesions. Shows what percentage of a team's possesions end in another possesion for that team.

POS- possesions. Calculated as 0.96*(field goal attempts + turovers + 0.44 * free throw attempts - offensive rebounds). The 0.96 constant accounts for team rebounds when a shot is missed but then tipped out of bounds by an defensive player. The 0.44 constant comes from the fact that roughly 44% of free throws end an offensive possesion.

PTS/POS- points per possesion.

STL%- steal percentage. Calculated as steals per opponents possesions. Shows what percent of a team's opponent's possesions end in a steal.

TS%- true shot percentage. Calculated as (2 * field goals made + 3 * threes made + free throws made) / (2 * field goal attempts + 0.44 * free throw attempts). The 0.44 constant comes from the fact that roughly 44% of free throws end an offensive possesion.
